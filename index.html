Jay Henderson, PhD
(he/any)
jayhend [at] mun.ca


I'm an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Memorial University of Newfoundland where I work on understanding fundamental user input paradigms and gestural interactions. I completed my PhD in the Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) Lab at the University of Waterloo, advised by Prof. Edward Lank. My research interests span a variety of domains within human-computer interaction, including learning input techniques, extended reality (AR/VR/MR), and 2D/3D interaction – with the overarching goal of understanding human behaviour while interacting with emerging technology.
