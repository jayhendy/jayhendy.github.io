Jay Henderson, PhD (he/they)
jay.henderson [at] carleton.ca


I'm a Postdoctoral Fellow in the School of Information Technology at Carleton University where I work on understanding fundamental user input and multi-user experiences in virtual reality (VR). I completed my PhD in the Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) Lab at the University of Waterloo, advised by the late Prof. Edward Lank. Though I'm interested in a ton of different areas of HCI and UX, at a high level, my research explores the design, development and assessment of interactive systems, with particular focus on facilitating learning and modelling user behaviour. Within such a multi-disciplinary field, I leverage techniques from human-computer interaction, machine learning, ergonomics, and psychology.
