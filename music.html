Music

Throughout my undergraduate degree (pre-transition) I wrote and recorded some original songs for three benefit albums as a part of the Conduct Becoming organization. You can have a listen here. In my spare time, I'm currently working on few self-produced songs that will be released shortly!


Credits:

Running Out (2016) written and performed by Jay Henderson. Produced by Stephen Buckley & Sadie Donahue in Sackville, NB.

The Time Traveller (2015) written by Jay Henderson & performed by Sydney Phelan, Anne Haley, & Jay Henderson. Produced by Justin McKiel & Stephen Buckley in Sackville, NB.

Autumn Rains (2015) written by Jonathan Whitaker & performed by Jonathan Whitaker & Jay Henderson. Produced by Justin McKiel & Stephen Buckley in Sackville, NB.

Stay (2014) written and performed by Laura Windhorst, Anne Haley, & Jay Henderson. Produced by Odum Abekah in Sackville, NB.
