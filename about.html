About


Hey! My name is Jay and I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. I was born and raised in the most easterly city of North America - St. John's, Newfoundland, Canada. I completed my undergraduate degree at Mount Allison University in May of 2016 with a Bachelor of Science Honours in Computer Science and minors in Mathematics and Psychology. Currently, I am a member of the Human Computer Interaction Lab under the supervision of Dr. Ed Lank.

Broadly, my research centers around expressive cross-device interaction. This includes, wearables (smartwatches, smartglasses, etc.), mobile devices and other ubiquitous commodity technology.

Outside of computer science, my interests extend to music, sports (hockey), and travelling.
